About Me

Thanks for taking a moment to learn about me. I have an International BA in Psychology from University College Dublin, where I spent one of my four years of study in Georgetown University, Washington D.C. During my undergraduate, I became very interested in the field of Human-Computer Interaction and conducted my final year research project in the UCD Media & Entertainment Lab, comparing performance on a visual search task in two and three-dimensions. My project was included in a larger body of work, and as a result I became a published author.

  • Read Paper

Since graduating I have worked as a tour guide, brand ambassador and even represented Ireland on the JET Programme teaching English in schools in Japan. These experiences have allowed me to really develop my interpersonal & communication abilities. In my spare time I have been teaching myself web development and design. Everything you see here (including the site itself) is a product of searching for resources, getting stuck in and learning by doing!

  • View CV

Now, I’m in a privileged position to be pursuing my passion with formal studies as I am currently pursuing a Higher Diploma in Interaction Design from Dublin City University. My ultimate goal is to get into the web development field so I can not only learn in the classroom, but in the real world too.
